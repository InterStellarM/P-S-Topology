Whether \( \mathbb{R}^\omega \) with box topology is normal (T4) is still an open question.

“It is not known whether \( \mathbb{R}^\omega \) is normal in the box topology. Mary-Ellen Rudin has shown that the answer is affirmative if one assumes the continuum hypothesis [RM]. In fact, she shows it satisfies a stronger condition called paracompactness.” — quoted from Exercise 32.5 in Munkres<
