Theorem 17.5

Let \(A\) be a subset of the topological space \(X\).

  1. \(x \in \overline{A}\) if and only if every open set \(U\) containing \(x\) intersects \(A\).
  2. If the topology of \(X\) is given by a basis, then \(x \in \overline{A}\) if and only if every basis element \(B\) containing \(x\) intersects \(A\).
