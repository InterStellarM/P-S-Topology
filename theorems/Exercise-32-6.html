Exercise 32.6

A space \(X\) is completely normal (T5) if and only if for every pair of separated subsets \(A, B \subseteq X\) (that is, \(\overline{A} \cap B = \varnothing\) and \(A \cap \overline{B} = \varnothing\)), there exist disjoint open sets of \(X\) containing \(A\) and \(B\), respectively.
