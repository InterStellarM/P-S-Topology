Lemma 21.2 (Sequence Lemma)

Let \(X\) be a topological space and let \(A \subseteq X\).

  1. (a) If there is a sequence of points of \(A\) converging to \(x\), then \(x \in \overline{A}\).
  2. (b) Conversely, if \(X\) is metrizable and \(x \in \overline{A}\), then there exists a sequence of points of \(A\) converging to \(x\).
