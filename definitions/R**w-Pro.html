\( \mathbb{R}^\omega \) with Product Topology

\( \mathbb{R}^\omega \) is the countable infinite product of \( \mathbb{R} \) with itself.

Definition: Let \( \{ X_\alpha \}_{\alpha \in J} \) be an indexed family of topological spaces. Product topology on \( \prod_{\alpha \in J}{X_\alpha} \) is generated by basis \( \prod_{\alpha \in J}{U_\alpha} \) and \( U_\alpha = X_\alpha \) except for finite many of \alpha. \( U_\alpha \) is open in \( X_\alpha \).
