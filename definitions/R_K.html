K-Topology \( \mathbb{R}_K \)

Definition: Let \( K = \{1,\tfrac{1}{2},\tfrac{1}{3},\dots\} \subseteq \mathbb{R} \). The K-topology on \(\mathbb{R}\) (denoted \(\mathbb{R}_K\)) is generated by the basis consisting of all sets of the form \( (a,b) \) and \( (a,b) \setminus K \), where \( a,b \in \mathbb{R} \) and \( a < b \).
