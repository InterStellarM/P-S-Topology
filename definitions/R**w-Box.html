\( \mathbb{R}^\omega \) with Box Topology

\( \mathbb{R}^\omega \) is the countable infinite product of \( \mathbb{R} \) with itself.

Definition: Let \( \{ X_\alpha \}_{\alpha \in J} \) be an indexed family of topological spaces. Box topology on \( \prod_{\alpha \in J}{X_\alpha} \) is generated by basis \( \prod_{\alpha \in J}{U_\alpha} \), where \( U_\alpha \) is open in \( X_\alpha \).
