Sorgenfrey Plane \( \mathbb{R}_l^2 \)

\( \mathbb{R}_l^2 \) is the product topology on \( \mathbb{R}_l \times \mathbb{R}_l \).

\(\)

Definition:

Let \((X, \mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y, \mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces. The product topology on \( X \times Y = \{(x,y) : x \in X, y \in Y\} \) is the topology generated by basis \(\mathcal{B}\) of the form \( U \times V \), where \(U \in \mathcal{T}_X,\ V \in \mathcal{T}_Y. \)
